WILLOUGHBY SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA



The Willoughby Symphony Orchestra (WSO) of Sydney commissioned a flyer for their new program for the fall 2023 season. I created a visual identity that aims to maintain the essence of their previous identity while refreshing its presentation. To achieve this goal, I created an easy to use shape library inspired by the cubist artistic movement that can be accessed and used anytime for future uses. This project was made in collaboration with Alphabet Studio, a famous graphic design studio based in Sydney and London.
