DIDACTIQUE DU FLE



The FLE (Français Langue Étrangère) MOOC aims to support worldwide french teachers by offering a course on learning different teaching perspectives. It is born from a collaboration between the University of Geneva and the OIF (Organisation Internationale de la Francophonie).
