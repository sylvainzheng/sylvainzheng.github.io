MAI AU PARC



Mai au Parc (May at the Park) is a free annual Swiss music festival held at Bernasconi Park (Grand Lancy, Geneva) from May 31st to June 2nd, 2024. It marks the beginning of the open-air festival season in Switzerland, with its wooded environment contributing to a warm and cozy atmosphere. The client commissioned an F4-sized poster, a website, banners, and animations for the campaign.

For this project, I chose to create a hand-drawn visual identity inspired by the dynamic energy of the festival, drawing from Keith Haring's distinctive art style. Colorful hands and fluid typography set against a warm magenta background evoke the main themes of Mai au Parc: rhythm, music, and diversity.
