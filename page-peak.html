PEAK SUNSCREEN



Peak is a sunscreen brand designed specifically for mountain sports enthusiasts, particularly hikers. The visual identity of its packaging centers around the concept of progression, reflected by its triangular and pointed shape reminiscent of mountains, evoking notions of speed and performance. The text is strategically arranged to provide stability to the object. Peak is mainly intended for a male audience who wishes to surpass themselves through demanding challenges.
