SHIMMER ENERGY BARS



Introducing Shimmer, a wholefood brand that focuses on energy bars made with 100% natural ingredients. By blending the sweetness of fruits and the crunchiness of nuts, Shimmer bars deliver a healthy and delicious snack that revitalises and nourishes the body. They are convenient and easily transportable anywhere. Shimmer is also more than just a snack; they are committed to sustainability and transparency, as evidenced by the minimal packaging made from recycled materials. Below is the list of all the different flavours available. Each bar comes with a mix of three different ingredients that give a unique and unforgettable taste.
