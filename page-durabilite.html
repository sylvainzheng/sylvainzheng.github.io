DURABILITÉ PROGRAMMÉE



"Planned Sustainability" is a board game where each player owns a company with the goal of repairing and extending the lifespan of everyday objects. The game is designed to teach children about the importance of sustainability. The name "Planned Sustainability" contrasts with the concept of "planned obsolescence". This project was a team effort by three people: Cloé P., Axell R., and myself.
