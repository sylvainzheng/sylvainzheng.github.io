CUISINE SANS +



“cuisine sans+” is a fusion recipe book. It explains the why and how to cook healthier meals and also provides descriptions of all the nutriments and their benefits to the body and mind. The book’s layout is created in a minimalist way to represent the absence of additives and chemicals, therefore, leaving only the healthy parts.
