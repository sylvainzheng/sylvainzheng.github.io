LUNAR NEW YEAR 2023



Reinterpretation is the main theme of this project. This Chinese New Year poster featuring the rabbit zodiac was created from a 3D model of a porcelain rabbit that I crafted using Blender, along with geometric Chinese characters. The poster diverges from the usual imagery associated with this period; instead, it portrays an industrial New Year with subdued lighting and a plastic-like portrayal of the animal. The rabbit here is depicted as a 'value' or a 'symbol.'

This poster represents a 'realistic' take on the Chinese New Year. The general aspect diverges from the conventional or traditional representations, instead the realistic approach tends to represent a more contemporary aesthetic by using subdued lighting, modern materials and less ornate.
