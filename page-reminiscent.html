REMINISCENT SERIF



Reminiscent Serif is a font I created, drawing inspiration from the Art Nouveau movement infused with an industrial aspect. Primarily designed for titles, its broad letters exude a sense of stability and strength. Each letter is constructed with precision using small blocks on a grid, incorporating ¼ circles for serifs and squares for the letterforms. Currently, Reminiscent Serif is employed in a personal game project called “Reminiscence” where it serves to enhance and complete the titles.
